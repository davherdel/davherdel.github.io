## 🎬 Featured Projects

### The Animation Saga — a two-part project
The dataset built in Part 1 trained the model in Part 2.

- 📺 **[TV Series Success Analysis](https://github.com/davherdel/TV-Series-Success-Analysis)**  
  *Business Intelligence & Predictive Analytics*  
  End-to-end ML on 3,495 animated TV shows across 75 years of industry history, enriched with IMDb data. Random Forest & XGBoost, with a Power BI dashboard.

- 🎬 **[Franchise Concept Analyzer](https://github.com/davherdel/Franchise-Concept-Analyzer)**  
  *Machine Learning Product Development*  
  A deployed XGBoost [Streamlit app](https://franchise-concept-analyzer.streamlit.app/) that scores the franchise potential of new show concepts, combining IMDb, Amazon, Netflix & YouTube signals.

### More Work

- 🔋 **[European Energy Transition](https://github.com/davherdel/Europe-Energy-Transition)**  
  *Dashboarding, Storytelling & Data Visualization*  
  25 years of European electricity generation analyzed and delivered as two interactive dashboards: a Tableau workbook and a custom-built HTML/CSS dashboard.

- 🎵 **[NeuroBeats by TheBeat](https://github.com/davherdel/NeuroBeats_by_TheBeat)**  
  *Marketing Analytics & Engagement Modeling*
